## 📝 Customization Guide

| Placeholder | What to Put |
|-------------|------------|
| `YOUR_LINKEDIN` | Full LinkedIn profile URL |
| `YOUR_PORTFOLIO` | Your portfolio website URL |
| `YOUR_EMAIL` | Your email address |
| `YOUR_USERNAME` | GitHub username (appears multiple times) |
| `YOUR_REPO_1` / `YOUR_REPO_2` | Your two best repository names |
| Typing SVG `lines=` | Your own descriptions, separated by `;` |

## 💡 Student-Specific Tips

1. **Add your GPA** only if it is above 3.5 / 8.0+ CGPA
2. **List internships** in the About section even if short
3. **Mention hackathon participation** — even if you didn't win
4. **Keep "Looking for" updated** — change it as your status changes
5. **Add your graduation year** — recruiters need this context
