## 💡 Tips for Choosing Badges

- ✅ Only add badges for skills you **genuinely know**
- ✅ Organize them into **logical groups** with headers
- ✅ Aim for **10-20 badges** max — more looks cluttered
- ❌ Do not add a badge just because it looks cool
- ❌ Do not list tools you only used once or twice
